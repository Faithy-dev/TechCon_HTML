Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon has grown from a local developer meetup into a global technology conference attracting thousands of attendees every year. From humble beginnings, it has become a hub for innovation, collaboration, and inspiration in the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to empower developers, entrepreneurs, and technology enthusiasts with knowledge, tools, and networking opportunities. We believe in inclusive innovation, continuous learning, and pushing boundaries in technology to improve the future for all.

Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace Award Winner - Dr. Sarah Chen

Dr. Chen is a pioneer in AI ethics and a champion for responsible machine learning. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a keynote on “AI for Humanity.”

Elon Turing - Cloud Infrastructure Engineer

Elon spoke at TechCon 2021 about building scalable cloud platforms and open-source contributions. He’s known for his work on real-time edge computing frameworks.

Rana Kassem - Tech for Social Good Advocate

Rana shared her journey of launching inclusive tech education platforms in underserved communities. Her talk in 2023 was one of the most inspiring of the decade.
